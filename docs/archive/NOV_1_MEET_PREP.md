#### Part 2: UNDERWATER KICKS (20 minutes) - MOST IMPORTANT!

**Goal**: Get comfortable with 6+ kicks and reaching 15 feet

**Set 1**: With fins (if you have them)
- [ ] **8 x 15 yards underwater dolphin kick** (on :45 rest)

**Set 2**: Without fins (race conditions)
- [ ] **8 x 15 yards underwater dolphin kick** (on :60 rest)

#### Part 3: STARTS FROM BLOCKS (15 minutes) - CRITICAL!

**Goal**: Explosive dive, deep entry, strong underwater

**If you have blocks**:
- [ ] **10-12 starts from blocks**
  - Parent says "Take your mark... GO!"
