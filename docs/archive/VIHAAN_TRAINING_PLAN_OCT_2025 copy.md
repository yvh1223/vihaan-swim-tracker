### Specific Drills - 50 FL SCY

#### 1. **Underwater Dolphin Kicks (Critical!)**
**Goal**: Build explosive underwater power
**Sets**:
- **4 x 15m underwater kick on back** (full streamline, no breath)
  - Focus: Fast, small, powerful kicks from the hips
  - Rest: 30 seconds between each
  - Target: Reach 12-15m before surfacing

- **4 x 25 FL with 5 dolphin kicks off each wall**
  - Count kicks to ensure exactly 5 strong kicks
  - Surface before the flags (5m mark)
  - Rest: 20 seconds

**Key Points**:
- Tight streamline (hands locked, biceps squeezing ears)
- Kick from hips, not knees
- Fast, small kicks (like a whip)

#### 2. **One-Arm Butterfly Drill**
**Goal**: Improve arm recovery and reduce drag
**Sets**:
- **6 x 25 (Right arm only, Left arm extended forward)**
  - Focus: High elbow recovery, fingertips skim water
  - Breathe every stroke
  - Rest: 15 seconds

- **6 x 25 (Left arm only, Right arm extended forward)**
  - Same focus points

- **2 x 25 Full stroke** (feel the difference)

**Key Points**:
- Keep hips high
- Extended arm stays streamlined
- Working arm recovers OVER water, not through it

#### 3. **Fast Hands Butterfly**
**Goal**: Increase stroke rate without sacrificing technique
**Sets**:
- **8 x 25 FL on :45**
  - Odd: FAST hands (quick recovery, powerful pull)
  - Even: Regular tempo with perfect technique
  - Focus: Maintain distance per stroke while increasing rate

**Key Points**:
- Quick recovery over water
- Powerful catch and pull
- Don't sacrifice body position for speed

#### 4. **Start Practice (ESSENTIAL for -0.15s)**
**Goal**: Explosive starts with deep underwater
**Sets**:
- **10 x 15m starts from blocks**
  - Push off HARD
  - Enter water at 45¬∞ angle (not flat)
  - Stay underwater with streamline + 5 dolphin kicks
  - Surface at 12-15m mark
  - Walk back and repeat

**Key Points**:
- Powerful leg drive off blocks
- Tight streamline (squeeze head between arms)
- Fast dolphin kicks before surfacing

#### 5. **Turn Practice**
**Goal**: Fast wall touch and explosive push-off
**Sets**:
- **6 x 50 FL focusing on turns**
  - Touch wall with TWO hands simultaneously
  - Tuck knees FAST, feet to wall
  - Push off on back or side (not belly)
  - 3 dolphin kicks before surfacing

**Key Points**:
- Two-hand touch (butterfly/breaststroke rule)
- Quick tuck and rotation
- Explosive push-off

### Specific Drills - 50 BR SCY

#### 1. **Breaststroke Pullout Perfection (CRITICAL!)**
**Goal**: Master the underwater pullout (biggest time saver!)
**Sets**:
- **8 x 25 BR focusing ONLY on the pullout**
  - Push off wall in streamline
  - Pull hands down to hips (big pull)
  - Hold streamline with arms at sides for 1 second
  - One strong breaststroke kick
  - Glide in streamline until you MUST breathe
  - Surface and take ONE stroke to wall
  - Rest: 30 seconds

**Target**: Reach 12-15m mark before surfacing

**Key Points**:
- BIG pull to hips (not just to chest)
- Hold streamline at sides (don't rush the kick)
- EXPLOSIVE breaststroke kick
- Maximum glide before surfacing

#### 2. **Two Kicks, One Pull Drill**
**Goal**: Develop powerful leg drive
**Sets**:
- **6 x 50 BR: 2 kicks per 1 pull** on 1:30
  - Pull once, kick
  - Glide in streamline
  - Kick again
  - Glide
  - Then pull again
  - Focus: Powerful kick, long glide

**Key Points**:
- Kick with feet turned OUT (like a frog)
- Heels come to butt, then SNAP feet outward
- Squeeze legs together at end of kick

#### 3. **Breaststroke Kick with Board**
**Goal**: Isolate and strengthen leg power
**Sets**:
- **8 x 25 kick with board** on :50
  - Streamline position on board
  - Powerful kicks
  - Focus on ankle flexibility and snap

**Key Points**:
- Feet flexed, turned outward
- Explosive kick with quick recovery
- Minimize knee bend (kick from hips)

#### 4. **Fast Tempo Breaststroke**
**Goal**: Reduce glide time, increase stroke rate
**Sets**:
- **4 x 50 BR on 1:20**
  - Count strokes (try to get 9-11 strokes total)
  - Quick hands (don't pause too long in glide)
  - Powerful kick every stroke

**Key Points**:
- Quick hands forward
- Minimize dead space in glide
- Every stroke is powerful

#### 5. **Start Practice with Pullout**
**Goal**: Perfect the start-to-first-stroke sequence
**Sets**:
- **10 x 15m starts** (focus on pullout)
  - Dive deep (hands enter at 45¬∞ angle)
  - Hold streamline down to depth
  - ONE perfect pullout
  - Surface around 12-15m
  - Take one stroke to "finish"

**Key Points**:
- Deep entry (not flat)
- Big pull to hips
- Explosive kick
- Surface close to flags

  - Don't breathe until you MUST
  - Target: 12-15m before first stroke

**Key Points**:
- Kick from hips (chest should rise and fall)
- Tight streamline (squeeze arms to ears)
- Fast rhythm (like a rapid drumbeat)

#### 2. **One-Arm Backstroke Drill**
**Goal**: Perfect arm path and high elbow catch
**Sets**:
- **6 x 25 (Right arm only, Left at side)**
  - Focus: Straight arm recovery (pinky up)
  - Deep catch (feel the water with whole forearm)
  - Rest: 15 seconds

- **6 x 25 (Left arm only, Right at side)**

- **2 x 25 Full stroke**

**Key Points**:
- Pinky finger exits first (thumb down)

**Goal**: Increase stroke rate without losing distance per stroke
**Sets**:
- **6 x 50 BK on 1:15**
  - Count strokes for first 25 (try for 13-15 strokes)
  - Second 25: Increase tempo but keep same stroke count
  - Focus: Quick arms, continuous motion

**Key Points**:
- Arms always moving (one enters as other exits)
- No pause at hips
- Maintain rotation with each stroke

#### 4. **Backstroke Starts (Big Time Saver!)**
**Goal**: Explosive underwater off the start
**Sets**:
- **10 x 15m backstroke starts**
  - Feet high on wall (toes at waterline)
  - Throw head back and UP (not just back)
  - Enter water with hands first
  - 8-10 dolphin kicks before first stroke
  - Surface around 15m

**Key Points**:
- Powerful leg drive
- Arch back, throw head back
- Tight streamline underwater
- Fast dolphin kicks

#### 5. **Flip Turn Practice**
**Goal**: Fast turns with strong push-off
**Sets**:
- **8 x 50 BK focusing on turns**
  - Flip over onto stomach
  - Plant feet and push off on BACK
  - 5-6 dolphin kicks before surfacing
  - First stroke should be at 10-12m mark

**Key Points**:
- Look for flags (know when to turn)
- Fast flip
- Push off on back (not on stomach)
- Strong dolphin kicks

#### 6. **Body Position Drill - Kick on Back**
**Goal**: Learn optimal body position
**Sets**:
- **4 x 50 kick on back** (no arms) on 1:30
  - Arms at sides OR in streamline
  - Focus: Head back, hips high
  - Eyes looking UP (not forward)
  - Steady flutter kick

**Key Points**:
- Water level at ears
- Hips at surface (don't sit)
- Toes pointed, fast flutter kick
