## üí™ KEY TECHNICAL TERMS TO REMEMBER

### Swimming Terminology
- **Streamline**: Arms straight overhead, hands stacked, body straight like arrow
- **Bilateral breathing**: Breathing on both sides (alternate left/right)
- **Breathing every 3/4**: Count strokes between breaths (1-2-3-breathe or 1-2-3-4-breathe)
- **Negative split**: Second 50 faster than first 50
- **High stroke rate/tempo**: Arms moving quickly with minimal pause
- **Even pace**: All 25s roughly same speed
- **Turnover**: How fast you complete one full stroke cycle

### Race Terminology
- **Off the wall/off the start**: Immediately after pushing off
- **Into the wall**: Approaching the wall (last few strokes)
- **Split time**: Time at 25y or 50y or 75y (not final time)
- **Build speed**: Gradually increase pace through the race
- **Controlled pace**: Fast but not all-out (80-85% effort)
- **Sprint/All-out**: Maximum effort (95-100%)

### Breaststroke Specific
- **Pullout**: Underwater pull to hips + one kick + glide
- **Glide phase**: Slide forward after kick (count "1-2")
- **Pull-breathe-kick-glide**: Timing sequence for BR
- **Narrow kick**: Heels together, knees shoulder-width

### Lane Position
- **Middle of lane**: Centered between lane lines (don't drift)
- **Why it matters**: Shortest distance, less turbulence
