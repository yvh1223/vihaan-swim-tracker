VIHAAN - 200 BACK & 200 BREAST MEET PREP

November 23, 2025

1-HOUR PRACTICE PLAN

WARM-UP (10 min)

  • 200 Choice easy
  • 4 x 50 Kick @ :55 (2 Back, 2 Breast)
  • 4 x 25 Drill @ :35 (2 Back, 2 Breast)

SET 1: BACKSTROKE (15 min)

4 x 50 Back @ 1:00 (Descend)

  • #1: 48 sec | #2: 46 sec | #3: 44 sec | #4: 42-43 sec

REST 1 min

2 x 100 Back @ 1:40 (Negative Split)

  • 1st 50: 44-45 sec | 2nd 50: 42-43 sec
FOCUS: 8-10 UW kicks, fast arms, high hips

SET 2: BREASTSTROKE (15 min)

4 x 50 Breast @ 1:10 (Descend)

  • #1: 52 sec | #2: 50 sec | #3: 48 sec | #4: 46 sec

REST 1 min

2 x 100 Breast @ 2:00 (Perfect Pullouts)

FOCUS: Hands to HIPS, hold 1 sec, BIG kick, glide to 15 ft

SET 3: RACE SIMULATION (10 min)

1 x 200 Back (broken) - 10 sec rest each wall

  • Splits: 43 / 45 / 46 / 46 = ~2:55-3:00

REST 2 min

1 x 200 Breast (broken) - 10 sec rest each wall

  • Splits: 50 / 54 / 55 / 55 = ~3:30-3:35

COOL DOWN (10 min)

  • 200 Easy choice
  • 4 x 25 Easy kick
  • 4 x 25 Easy swim (2 Back, 2 Breast)

PARENT WATCH LIST

BACKSTROKE

Good Signs:

  • Long UW kicks (5-6+) each wall
  • Arms moving continuously
  • Hips at surface
  • Negative splits on 100s

Warning Signs:

  • Coming up too early (<5 kicks)
  • Arms pausing/slowing
  • Hips dropping
  • 2nd 50 much slower than 1st

BREASTSTROKE

Good Signs:

  • Hands going to HIPS on pullout
  • Surfacing at flags (12-15 ft)
  • Long glide each stroke
  • 8-10 strokes per 50

Warning Signs:

  • Hands only to chest on pullout
  • Surfacing early (<10 ft)
  • Rushing strokes (no glide)
  • 12+ strokes per 50
