## Where You'll Find the Time

### For ALL Events - The Big 5

These **5 things** will make you faster in EVERY event:

#### 1. üåä Underwater Kicks (Biggest Time Saver!)
**What it means**: After you dive and after every turn, stay underwater longer with powerful kicks.

**How much time you'll save**: 0.5 to 1.5 seconds PER RACE!

**Why it works**: Moving underwater is faster than swimming on top of the water because there's no wave resistance.

**Your goal**:
- After starts: 6-10 kicks before surfacing (reach the flags - 15 feet)
- After turns: 4-6 kicks before surfacing (reach 10-12 feet)

---

#### 2. üöÄ Explosive Starts
**What it means**: Dive hard, enter the water deep, and stay streamlined.

**How much time you'll save**: 0.3 to 0.7 seconds!

**Why it works**: A good start can beat swimmers who have better strokes because you build speed immediately.

**Your goal**:
- Powerful leg drive off the blocks
- Enter water at an angle (like an arrow, not a belly flop)
- Tight streamline with arms locked over your head

---

#### 3. ‚ö° Perfect Turns
**What it means**: Fast flip (or touch), quick feet to wall, explosive push-off.

**How much time you'll save**: 0.2 to 0.5 seconds per turn!

**Why it works**: Slower turns break your momentum. Fast turns keep your speed.

**Your goal**:
- Don't breathe the last 2 strokes going into the wall
- Quick flip or two-hand touch
- Push off HARD with underwater kicks

---

#### 4. üèÉ Fast Stroke Rate
**What it means**: Move your arms faster without pausing.

**How much time you'll save**: 0.5 to 1.5 seconds!

**Why it works**: The more strokes you take per second, the faster you go (without losing distance per stroke).

**Your goal**:
- Keep your arms moving continuously
- Don't glide too long between strokes
- Quick recovery over the water

---

#### 5. üí™ Strong Finishes
**What it means**: Your last 3 strokes should be your FASTEST strokes.

**How much time you'll save**: 0.1 to 0.3 seconds!

**Why it works**: Many swimmers slow down at the end. You can pass them!

**Your goal**:
- Don't breathe the last 2 strokes
- Sprint to the wall
- Lunge and touch with a long reach

## Important Things to Remember

### 1. Technique > Speed (at your age)
Perfect technique now will make you MUCH faster when you're older. Don't sacrifice good form just to go fast.

### 2. Underwaters Are EVERYTHING
If you only practice ONE thing from this plan, practice underwater kicks. They're the biggest time saver!

### 3. Every Turn Matters
In a 50, you have 1 turn. In a 100, you have 1 turn. Make it count! A bad turn can cost you 0.5 seconds.

### 4. Don't Breathe Into Walls
The last 2 strokes before every wall = NO BREATHING. This keeps your speed up.

### 5. Last 3 Strokes = FASTEST
Finish strong! Sprint the last 3 strokes to the wall.

### 6. Rest and Recover
You need 9-10 hours of sleep every night. Your body grows and gets stronger when you sleep!

### 7. Have Fun!
Swimming should be fun! These goals are exciting challenges, not pressure.

## Mental Tips for Racing

### Before the Race
1. **Visualize**: See yourself doing perfect start, turns, and finish
2. **Breathe**: Take 3 deep breaths to calm nerves
3. **Focus**: Think about YOUR race, not other swimmers

### During the Race
1. **Start strong**: Explosive off the blocks!
2. **Stay long**: Don't rush, swim tall
3. **Attack the turn**: Make it fast!
4. **Finish faster**: Last 3 strokes are fastest!

### After the Race
1. **Check your time**: Did you PR (Personal Record)?
2. **Think positive**: What did you do well?
3. **Learn**: What can you improve next time?
4. **Celebrate**: Every PR deserves celebration!
